    Booking Policy

    We understand that plans can change, but to avoid any charges, please cancel at least 12 hours before your scheduled padel court booking time.

    Cancellations made within 12 hours of the booking time will result in a full charge of the booking fee, which will be split among the players included in the reservation. If some players are missing, the booking fee will be charged to the person who made the reservation.

    By renting a padel racket from us, you agree to handle it with care during your play. It is important to avoid actions that could cause damage to the racket.

    In the event that the racket is broken while in your possession, you will be responsible for a fee of $50.

    Please acknowledge this agreement before proceeding with the rental. Thank you for your cooperation in helping us maintain our equipment.

    In the event of rain or other weather conditions that may disrupt your court booking, please call the front desk before your reservation to confirm if the court is in playable condition. Otherwise, the club will determine at its discretion whether the court is playable or if it will be considered a rainout.

    To cancel your reservation, please connect to your account at padelclubaustin.com or Padel Club Austin APP

    **ALL ON TIME CANCELATIONS AND/OR RAINOUTS WILL BE ISSUED TO CLUB CREDITS**
